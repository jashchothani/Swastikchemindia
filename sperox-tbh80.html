Sperox TBH-80

Tert-Butyl Hydroperoxide (80%)
CONCENTRATED SOLUTION

Sperox TBH-80 is a high-purity, volatile, clear liquid solution of Tert-Butyl Hydroperoxide (80%). It offers superior oxidizing power and high thermal stability compared to other organic peroxides. It is widely used in chemical synthesis (Sharpless epoxidation) and as a polymerization initiator.

Chemical Name Tert-Butyl Hydroperoxide (TBHP)
Concentration 80% (Aqueous Solution)
Appearance Clear, Colorless Liquid
Active Oxygen ~14.2% Min
Key Characteristics High Purity, High Thermal Stability
Applications Chemical Synthesis, Polymerization Initiator, Oxidizing Agent
