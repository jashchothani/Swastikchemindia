Our Heritage

From Vision to Reality

The journey of Swastik Chemical India is a testament to resilience and vision. Founded by Late Sh. Kishore Thakkar as an agro-based enterprise, the company was built on the values of honesty and community service.

In 1998, under the dynamic leadership of Mr. Bharat Thakkar, the company pivoted to the chemical sector. Transforming a traditional business into a modern chemical powerhouse, we now serve industries across India with the same ethical foundation laid decades ago.
