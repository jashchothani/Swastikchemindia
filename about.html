Who We Are

A Tradition of Chemical Excellence

Swastik Chemical India is a specialized manufacturer and marketer of organic peroxides, serving diverse industrial applications across India. Our foundation is built on integrity, safety, and consistent quality. We have evolved into a focused and reliable partner for the polymer, rubber, coatings, and specialty chemical industries.

Our Journey & Legacy

From Agro to Industrial Innovation

The roots of Swastik Chemical India were planted by the visionary Kishore Thakkar (Seated), who originally established the family's reputation in agro-based enterprise. His wisdom and adherence to ethical business practices laid the groundwork for our future.

In 1998, under the dynamic leadership of Bharat Kishore Thakkar (Standing), the company pivoted into the specialized chemical sector.

Today, that blend of traditional values and modern chemical expertise drives every decision we make, ensuring we remain a partner you can trust.
